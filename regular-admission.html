ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR B.SC. (F.C.SC.) (HONOURS)

FAMILY AND COMMUNITY RESOURCE MANAGEMENT - HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT

  • The courses in the First Year of the B.Sc. (Honors) Regular Programme are common for all the five streams of the Faculty of Family and Community Sciences namely Clothing and Textiles, Extension and Communication, Family and Community Resource Management, Foods and Nutrition and Human Development and Family Studies.
  • At the Second-Year level, choice and merit-based selection are made for all the streams of the Faculty of Family and Community Sciences including two specializations of the Family and Community Resource Management, namely Hospitality Management and Interior Design.


FAMILY AND COMMUNITY RESOURCE MANAGEMENT - INTERIOR DESIGN

  • The educational qualification of the candidate seeking admission in B.Sc. Honours in Interior Design (HPP) should be Minimum 10 + 2 pass with English as a compulsory subject or should have studied through English as a medium of instruction
    • OR
  • Diploma in Architecture/Civil Engineering after 10th (minimum 3-Years duration) 39 from a recognized University.
  • The admissions are open for both male and female candidates who possess the required educational qualifications.
  • The number of the seat for said program is 60.
  • The candidate should register for admissions online and carefully read other related details available on following website..

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR M.SC. (F.C.SC.), DEPARTMENT OF FCRM.

FAMILY AND COMMUNITY RESOURCE MANAGEMENT - HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME

  • The candidate holding a Bachelor‟s degree in any of the following disciplines from The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda or any other University or recognized Institution, shall be considered eligible for admission to M.Sc. (F.C.Sc.) in Family and Community Resource Management (Hospitality and Resource Management).
    • ➡B. F. C. Sc. (Gen) or B. A. (Home) Degree with General Home Science, 10+2+3 pattern of Educationwith 55% or above marks or equivalent grade.
    OR
    ➡B. F. C. Sc. / B. F. C. Sc. (Home) / B. F. C. Sc. (Honours) Degree in FCRM, 10+2+3 pattern of Education with 55% or above marks or equivalent grade.
  • Admission to M.Sc. F.C.Sc. Degree Program in the Department is on Merit, based on the marks/grade obtained at the qualifying entrance examination and interview.
  • There will be an interview and written test for those who meet the minimum requirement of higher second class or an equivalent grade at the qualifying examination on the strength of which admission is sought.
  • However, the candidates selected for admission would be required to clear pre-requisite courses prescribed by the department if needed.

FAMILY AND COMMUNITY RESOURCE MANAGEMENT - INTERIOR DESIGN

  • The candidate holding a Bachelor‟s degree in any of the following disciplines from The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda or any other University or recognized Institution, shall be considered eligible for admission to M.Sc. (F.C.Sc.) in Family and Community Resource Management (Interior Design).
    • B. F. C. Sc. (Gen) or B. A. (Home) Degree with General Home Science, 10+2+3 pattern of Education with 55% or above marks or equivalent grade.
    OR
    B. F. C. Sc. / B. F. C. Sc. (Home) / B. F. C. Sc. (Honours) Degree in FCRM, 10+2+3 pattern of Education with 55% or above marks or equivalent grade.
  • Admission to M.Sc. F.C.Sc. Degree Program in the Department is on Merit, based on the marks/grade obtained at the qualifying entrance examination and interviews. There will be interview and written test for those who meet the minimum requirement of higher second class or an equivalent grade at the qualifying examination on the strength of which admission is sought. Students seeking admission in M.Sc. (F.C.Sc.) Degree Program with specialization in Interior Design will be required to also clear a drawing Aptitude test, along with the written test andinterview. However, the candidates selected for admission would be required to clear pre-requisite courses prescribed by the department if needed.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR PH.D. AT DEPARTMENT OF FCRM.

The candidates having the Post Graduate Degree in FCRM/FRM/HM/Home are eligible for the admission for Ph.D. program in Department of Family and Community Resource Management. However, for the Ph.D. admission besides the above degree the candidates should have cleared either of the following examination
Ph.D.Entrance Test (PET) exam in the subject FCRM/FRM/Home Management conducted periodically by The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda.
UGC-JRF/NET inthe subject FCRM/FRM/Home Management /related subject.
GUJ SLET in the subject FCRM/FRM/Home Management /related subject.
